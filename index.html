About AthloX

Founded in 2023 by a team of former collegiate athletes and fintech experts, AthloX emerged from a shared vision to revolutionize how athletes are supported and compensated during their academic careers. Our founders experienced firsthand the financial challenges faced by student-athletes and saw an opportunity to create a platform that would bridge the gap between athletic potential and financial success.

At AthloX, our mission is to empower athletes to achieve their full potential by providing innovative financial solutions that reward performance and dedication. We believe in creating a sustainable ecosystem where athletes can focus on excellence while investors can participate in their journey to success. Our core values of transparency, integrity, and innovation drive everything we do, ensuring that both athletes and investors thrive in our community.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the investment process work?

Athletes create profiles showcasing their achievements and potential. Investors can browse profiles, analyze performance metrics, and make investment decisions. Returns are calculated based on predetermined performance indicators and distributed automatically through our secure platform.

What are the eligibility requirements?

Athletes must be currently enrolled in an accredited college or university and maintain NCAA compliance. Investors must be accredited and pass our verification process. Both parties must complete our comprehensive onboarding process.

How are performance metrics tracked and verified?

We use a combination of official athletic department data, verified competition results, and our proprietary tracking system. All metrics are independently verified and updated in real-time on our platform.

What security measures are in place?

AthloX employs bank-level encryption, secure payment processing, and regular security audits. All transactions are monitored 24/7, and we maintain comprehensive insurance coverage for both athletes and investors.

What happens if an athlete gets injured?

Our platform includes injury protection provisions and performance adjustment protocols. Investment terms are automatically adjusted during recovery periods, and we provide comprehensive support throughout the rehabilitation process.
